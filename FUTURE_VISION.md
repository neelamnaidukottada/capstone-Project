- Deliver a reliable end-to-end autonomous campaign workflow.
- Keep human approval for strategy and budget as mandatory safety controls.
- Provide real-time visibility and persistent campaign state.
- Demonstrate usable reporting output for stakeholder review.

## Out-of-Scope (Now)
- Full enterprise governance and role hierarchy.
- Live channel publishing and spend sync with ad networks.
- Deep attribution modeling and predictive budget planning.
- Integration with th Media like Linkdin APIs, Google Ads API. 
- Native mobile applications.

## Stretch Goals (If Core Is Green)
- Lightweight what-if simulation before budget approval.
- Improved report exports with richer PDF formatting.
- Campaign templates and reusable strategy presets.
- Initial recommendation memory across campaign runs.

## North-Star Direction
Evolve from a guided autonomous assistant into a dependable campaign operating system where humans set intent, agents execute coordinated tasks, and governance remains transparent and auditable.
